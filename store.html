About Me

I have worked for over 30 years as an HR executive. I have counseled, coached, and worked with positions in the C suite and middle managers. I have the ability to connect with others and guide them in making the decisions that are best for them. I have run successful HR teams and implemented policies and procedures that benefit both the organization and its employees. I understand what it’s like to work in the corporate world: the demands on your time, working for the goals of the organization, being the employee advocate, and finding work-life balance.

With my experience and training, together, we can do great things for your leaders and ultimately for your organization’s success. We can unlock their skills and talents. I am the missing key!

Credentials

I am a certified executive coach who trained with the World Coach Institute. I became a coach because of my passion for nurturing great leaders. Good leaders have the qualities and skills to run successful companies. I want to help leaders understand their value in the organization and their impact on the employees and the company’s success. I also hold the highest certification for HR professionals, the SHRM-SCP.
